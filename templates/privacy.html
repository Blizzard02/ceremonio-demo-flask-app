Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ceremonio ("we", "us", "our") collects and processes personal data when you use Ceremonio (the "Service"), in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Greek law (Law 4624/2019). By using the Service you acknowledge this Policy.

1. Who we are (Data Controller)

Ceremonio is the controller of personal data processed through the Service. You can reach us at ceremioteam@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: your email address and an encrypted (hashed) password.
  • Event data you provide: information you enter to organise your event, which may include names, phone numbers, email addresses, RSVP responses, dietary preferences, seating arrangements, budget and supplier details, playlist, and bank/IBAN details if you choose to add them.
  • Guest responses: when guests use an invitation link, the RSVP and related answers they submit.
  • Technical data: a strictly necessary session cookie to keep you logged in, and standard server logs.

3. If you enter other people's data

If you add personal data about third parties (for example, your guests), you confirm that you have a lawful basis to do so and will inform them where appropriate. We process that data on your behalf to provide the Service.

4. Why we process it (legal bases)

  • To provide the Service and your account — performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • To keep the Service secure and functioning — our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • For optional data you choose to add (e.g. IBAN) — your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you may withdraw at any time.

5. Cookies

We use a single essential session cookie required for login. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your data. We use third-party infrastructure to host the Service, and may use third-party services to render certain optional features (for example, generating a QR-code image from an IBAN you enter). Such providers process data only as needed to provide the Service.

7. International transfers

Our hosting or service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where that occurs, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses).

8. Retention

We keep your account and event data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can delete your data through the Service or request deletion by emailing us.

9. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, to data portability, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact ceremioteam@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (Αρχή Προστασίας Δεδομένων Προσωπικού Χαρακτήρα, www.dpa.gr).

10. Security

Passwords are stored hashed and the Service is served over HTTPS. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

11. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

12. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time; the "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

13. Contact

For any privacy question or request: ceremioteam@gmail.com.
